About Us

Director

Diamond Francis is the Director of DBF Media, a dynamic advertising agency. With over four years of experience as a freelance graphic designer, she has gained extensive knowledge in the commercial market. Diamond specializes in graphic design, packaging design, website development, and advertising, acting for both national and local clients.

Fully retained as a regional agent, her expertise spans various industries, combining creativity and technical precision. Her academic background includes a degree from Coventry University and an MSc in Computing from the University of Sunderland, equipping her with the skills to deliver innovative digital solutions.

We expand along with our clients!
